## Community scope

The project is especially interested in practical feedback from AI infrastructure builders, data platform teams, analytics engineers, and product teams evaluating governed agent workflows.

Good community contributions usually include:

- A concrete data workflow, datasource, or analysis task.
- Reproduction steps, sample schema, screenshots, logs, or trace output.
- A clear distinction between current behavior, expected behavior, and product ideas.
- No secrets, production credentials, customer data, or private business records.

## Working principles

DataFoundry docs and examples should make it clear which capabilities are already implemented, which workflows are experimental, and which ideas belong on the roadmap. Community discussions are most useful when they stay evidence-based and reproducible.
